Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?
Register here. Your project facilitators may have added your email address already; if so, you'll be able to use Project Central right away. Otherwise, you'll get an email once your membership is approved and you can log in.
I forgot my login/password, what should I do?
If you know your login (the email you used to sign up), you can reset your password here.

If you don't remember the email you used, or no longer have access to it, you'll need to contact an admin.
How does the giving project news work?
The news section shares successes of giving project members. Whenever you complete a step, it is shared with other people in your project via the news feed.

What is shared: the date, your first name, a general description of what happened ("talked to", "asked" or "got a pledge") and the amount pledged if applicable.

Not shared: the contact's name or anything about them, your step description.

If you have any questions or concerns about this information being shared, please contact us! You can do so anonymously if you'd like - see below.
Can I access Project Central on my mobile phone?
Yes, Project Central can be accessed on mobile devices through a web browser. We've attempted to keep the design and layout relatively small-screen-friendly. We welcome any feedback on mobile usability.
Does Project Central work for people in more than one giving project?
Yes, it supports members being in multiple GPs.

When you register, pick one of the giving projects you're currently in - it doesn't matter which. Once you're signed up, there will be a "your projects" link at the bottom of the page. Go there to register for another GP. Each membership requires an approval from one of the project leaders.

Project Central will only show you one GP at a time and your contacts are unique to each project. To change which GP you're currently working in, use the same projects link at the bottom of the page. Clicking any link in your list of projects will set that as your "current" GP.
